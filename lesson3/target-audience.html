Target Audience

This website is for anyone connected to Smithfield, NC. Whether a current resident wanting to keep up with events, a business owner wanting to network with other businesses, or even a family looking to become part of our community!

Personas

Occupation: Homemaker

Use for Site: Wants to be part of a local community and be up to date on events.

Occupation: Local Business Owner

Use for Site: Wants to network with other local businesses and market himself to the community.

Scenarios

  1. How can I be more involved in building up the community?
  2. How can I stay up to date on community events?
  3. Who can I network with in the area to build my business?
  4. How can I support local businesses in my community?
  5. What is Smithfield's history and culture?
