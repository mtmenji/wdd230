Bountiful Foods

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to market the Bountiful Foods brand and business. The goal is to promote customer traffic into the main shop, but also raise awareness of the food trucks around the area.

Target Audience

Our target audience is local business enthusiasts that take pride in locally grown fruits!
